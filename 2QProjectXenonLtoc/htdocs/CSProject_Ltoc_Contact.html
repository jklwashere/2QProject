What is Bettearth?

Bettearth is a nonprofit campaigning organization aiming to inform more and more people about the dangerous effects of climate change. Based in the Philippines, the founder of Bettearth is no stranger to global warming/climate change and its consequences.
Bettearth is also determined to provide up-to-date, accurate data about climate all around the world. These includes changes in global temperature, natural disasters, sea level rise, glacier and ice sheet surface area fluctuation, and more.

For any concerns regarding the organization, please contact us at contact@bettearth.com
