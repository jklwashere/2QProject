How can I help the fight against climate change?

Well, you can first help these organizations by informing others of the effects of climate change.
You can also perform activities that can help the environment (community clean-up, tree-planting, and more).

IPCC

EDF

Greenpeace

The International Panel on Climate Change (IPCC), established in 1988 by the World Meteorological Organization, aims to help governments around the globe with decisions and data regarding climate change.

The Environmental Defense Fund (EDF) is a nonprofit organization whose mission is to help "preserve the natural systems on which all life depends".

Greenpeace is "an independent global campaigning organization" determined on preserving the diverse ecosystems and natural world of Earth.
